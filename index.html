Gout Compass

Gout flare risk changes throughout the day. Your plan should too.

Gout Compass is a dynamic wellness canvas for staying ahead of gout flare risk. It brings hydration, Apple Health signals, meals, labs, and symptoms into one adaptive daily view, then surfaces the right next step at the right time.

Hydration First

Keep water intake visible all day with goals that adapt to your current risk level.

Know Your Risk

Understand whether today looks Optimal, Caution, or Critical using Apple Watch and Apple Health signals.

Meal Co-Pilot

Check restaurant meals for purine risk before you order.

Track Your Labs

Capture uric acid results from lab reports and follow trends over time.

AI Insights

Get personalized wellness guidance that updates as your day changes.
